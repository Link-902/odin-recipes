Chili

How to make chili

This recipe will show you how to make chili, a hearty and delicious meal.

Ingredients list

  • Ground Beef
  • Kidney Beans
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Green Peppers
  • Celery
  • Jalapeno
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin

Recipe Steps

  1. First get all your ingredients ready
    • Dice all vegetables
    • Open all canned goods
    • Measure all spices
  2. Preheat your pot and cook ground beef until well browned then remove
  3. Add all the vegetable and cooked until softened
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, spices, ground beef, and kidney beans and mix throughly
  5. Simmer for one to two hours
  6. Serve in a bowl and enjoy
