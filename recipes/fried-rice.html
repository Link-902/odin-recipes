Fried Rice

How to make fried rice

This recipe will show you how to make a simple yet delicious version of fried rice, a dish everyone can enjoy.

List of ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Day-old Rice
  • Oil

Recipe Instructions

  1. Begin by cracking and whisking your eggs
  2. Heat pan, add oil and scramble eggs
  3. Remove from pan and set aside
  4. Add more oil and begin cooking Rice
  5. Cook until browned then add eggs back to pan
  6. Saute everything together
  7. Plate and serve
