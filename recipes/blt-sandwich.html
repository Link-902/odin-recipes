BLT Sandwich

How to make a BLT Sandwich

This recipe will show you how to make a great BLT sandwich

Ingredients

  • Loaf of bread
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Cooked bacon

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Cook the bacon until crispy
  3. Slice the lettuce and tomatos
  4. Layer the ingredients in this order
    1. Bread
    2. Mayo
    3. Lettuce
    4. Tomotos
    5. Bacon
    6. Mayo
    7. Bread
  5. Slice on the diagional and serve
